Hammerfest, Norway

Price Range: 200-500€

Welcome to Hammerfest – one of the northern-most places in Norway! Here you'll see the beautiful Arctic Ocean with its cold waves and fabulous greenery covering the rocky cliffs. In the middle of the strait you can also stumble upon a small fishing town, near which everyone can find something new!

Krka National Park, Croatia

Price Range: 1500-25000€

There's no better destination than a peaceful park with unbelievably beautiful scenery! The most popular national park in Croatia is located within a short drive from the ocean.
